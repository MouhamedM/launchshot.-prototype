How long does the process take?

The entire process, from uploading photos to receiving your final high-resolution headshots, typically takes about 20-30 minutes.

What kind of photos do I need to upload?

We recommend 4 to 10 clear, high-quality selfies. Avoid heavy filters, extreme shadows, or photos with other people for the best AI training results.

Are my photos secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. Your original uploaded photos are only used to train your personal AI model and are deleted after a short period.
